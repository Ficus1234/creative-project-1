The Ficus Lyrata needs about 3 cups of water a week.
Most Fiddle Leaf Fig plants you'll see are relatively small, since they're houseplants; however, in their natural habitat, they can grow to be 50 feet tall!
If these plants are well cared for, they can grow up to two feet per year.
What most people don't know about these plants before purchasing is that they are some of the most finicky houseplants you can buy. If they don't get enough water (or if they get too much), and if conditions aren't right, these plants will begin to die.
